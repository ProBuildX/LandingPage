About us

BuildSphere is a software product that is dedicated to transforming the construction industry through technological innovation. Our mission is to provide our customers with the tools they need to optimize their processes, increase efficiency , and achieve better results.

Mission and Vision

Mission

Promote digital transformation in the construction industry, providing innovative solutions that improve project management, optimize resources and facilitate communication between different stakeholders. We seek to be the catalyst for change towards more efficient, sustainable and profitable practices in the sector.

Vision

To be global leaders in the field of technological solutions for construction, establishing new standards of efficiency, sustainability and quality in projects of any size. We strive to create a future where technology and construction advance hand in hand, overcoming current challenges and opening the way to innovations that benefit both companies and the environment.

Features

Materials & Machinery Management

Control material stock in real-time, plan purchases accurately, and reduce waste. Monitor the condition of your equipment, schedule preventive maintenance and increase its useful life.

Project planning and monitoring

Set specific goals and objectives for each phase of the project and make sure everyone involved is aligned. Track the progress of the project accurately and detect deviations or potential risks to take timely action.

Smart Inventory System

Predict material needs accurately and ensure the availability of the necessary resources in a timely manner. Streamline material purchases, avoid excess inventory, and reduce waste.
